About Us

The UNDER 30 CEO’S INITIATIVE is a project and a mandate committed to stir a business and financial revolution geared towards the rapid sustainable development of Africa. We are empowered, sponsored and committed to train 10,000 young Africans in entrepreneurship and financial education in the next five years. We are not the only organization doing this, but we are the most committed and dedicated-this is our life and passion.

The UNDER30 CEO’s INITIATIVE will gather over 500 young potential entrepreneurs, financial educators and leaders across Africa to be trained, inspired and challenged on yearly basis, to become change makers and to contribute toward creating a better African economy.

We aim to be a centre of excellence for entrepreneurship and financial education across Africa, by strengthen our capacity, initiative and partnerships with the Sustainable Development Goal of Africa and its drivers.
