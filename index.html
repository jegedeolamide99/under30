Our Core Values

T

Traction

This means the power needed to pull something along a surface. Business success and financial independence are things that we need power to attract and achieve. We therefore, believe that knowledge can be a major power to empower young Africans.

R

Responsibilty

For Africa to develop and harness her full potentials there is a need for collective and individual responsibility. “I respond base on my ability and you respond based on your ability”. It is this responsibility that is driving us into creating and pursuing this dream and project.

A

Acceleration

We are mighty but little, glorious but callous; this is not Africa. We need to grow fast in our minds, our technology, our man power and our business and financial know-how. This course we stand for-Under 30 CEO’s Initiative.

I

Innovation

Innovation is a currency in our present century. We aim to bring out the creativity and innovative thinking capacity imbedded in our youths for the rapid sustainable development of Africa.

L

Leadership Excellence

We believe every true leader should be business oriented and financially intelligent. This cause we stand to achieve and impact to the youths of Africa.

Our Mission

“To challenge young minds in creating innovative business ideas for the rapid sustainable development of Africa”.

Our Vision

“To professionally instruct, train and inspire potential entrepreneur, managers, leaders and individuals to become masters of money and their businesses".

Our Goals

  • To provide business and financial know-how, through the business and financial conference.
  • To create more practical oriented entrepreneurs and financial experts.
  • To train and empower new generational man power with global trends.
  • To encourage African leaders to champion sustainable development through entrepreneurship and financial education.

Our Commitment

  • To train 10,000 young entrepreneurs and financial experts in the next (5 years).
  • To empower 250 of the trained 10,000 through collaboration and partnership with other organizations.
  • To make 50 of the 250 empowered businesses become global.
  • To collaborate with the sustainable development goal.
