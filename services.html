Our Services

Business Training and Consulting

Apart from our commitment to train, instruct and inspire young Africans (youth) to use their creative potentials and ideas to spot market opportunities and establish businesses for development, we also train and creatively provide business advice for small-scale, medium scale and large scale firms and companies.

Financial Education

In partnership with the number one Sustainable Development Goal (i.e. No poverty). The Under 30 CEO’s Initiative has committed herself to train and educate young Africans in the basics and dynamics of money. Government will not share money for every citizen to reduce or eradicate poverty but they can invest in educating citizens out of poverty. This is our goal and vision.

Customer Relation Training

Business in the 21st century and beyond will be dependent on good customer relation. Profit which is the primary objective of all businesses is a function of the quantity and quality of customers. That is, the more customers a business generates, the more money (profit) it will produce. Therefore, you need us to professionally and strategically connect your products and services to the right customers and clients.

Awards & Grants

Several young Africans have discovered wonderful and brilliant business ideas and projects but lack the resources to materialize them. This is why we are passionate to connect young Africans with business Awards, Competitions and Grants floating around for the African youth. What you don’t know they said is bigger than you. Therefore, get your biggest opportunities through the Under 30 CEO’s Initiative Awards and Grants Connect.
