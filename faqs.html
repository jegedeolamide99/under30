This is the major channel through which the Under 30 CEO’s Initiative performs its obligations in line with the Sustainable Development Goal to African youths for the rapid development of Africa.
The business and financial conference is a yearly program aimed at training, instructing and inspiring youths across Africa to become agents of social change.
Africa’s development is only possible when we Africans build and develop ourselves. This is the reason why the conference focuses on gathering young potential entrepreneurs and financial independence seekers across Africa to be trained and transformed into change makers.

The business and financial conference is open to all young Africans who are passionate about entrepreneurship and financial independence leadership and are ready to create a better Africa for the Africans to come. There is no gender, age, education and religious barriers, it is accessible by all African, just sign up and register for the conference. See you there live.
